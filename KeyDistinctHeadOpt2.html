Cladoceran: Option 2: Distinct Head

This Cladoceran has a distinct head region, but no indent in the back

Option 1

Large, conical head, no crest

2nd antennae are enlarged “buff looking arms”

Body is translucent and it looks like a rib-cage inside.

No spine!

Distal setae may be visible

1st antennae may appear as tusks

Option 2

Small, conical head. May have crest

2nd antennae are not enlarged, may not be visible

May have a distal spine

May have a beak like structure inferior to eye spot

May have a pointed crest on head
