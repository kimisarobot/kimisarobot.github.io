Welcome to the ARC Project's Training Website.

The goal of this website is to help those learning to ID zooplankton from the North Delta and Suisun Marsh region. Here you'll find a list of all the zooplankton and other organisms we ID, a key to guide you through IDing, useful terms to use when discussing zooplankton, and photos to help you with tricky ID situations.
