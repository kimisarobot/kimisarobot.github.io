Short 1st Antennae

How is their body shaped? What do the rami look like?

Option 1

Tiny, diamond-shaped body

Long, straight urosome that splits into rami far down

Option 2

Tear-shaped body

Ursome split pretty close to the prosome

Option 3

Tear-shaped or relatively straight body

Ursome segmented and just two rami extending from the end of the prosome
