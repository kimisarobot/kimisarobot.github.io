ID Guide Walkthrough

This section will walk you through how I ID zoops. This is not the definitive way to ID zoops!

Each page will provide you with different options to choose from. Select the option that best fits what you're looking at. Your specimen may not have all the characteristics listed under an option, but that's okay. Zooplankton of the same genus are variable, or they may loose characteristics during processing. Depending on the option you choose, it will direct you to another page that will refine your options until you come to the correct identification.

Have fun zooping it up!
