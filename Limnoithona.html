Characteristics:

Calanoid copepod

Segmented prosome w/ legs attached

Sausage-like body

Antennae about the length of body

Rami are deep split in urosome, usually straight

Adult males can have curly antennae and bent fifth legs

If gavid, one egg sac in middle
